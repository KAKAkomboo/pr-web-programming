Dark Souls

The Age of Fire is dying out

The fire dies down, and darkness envelops the world. The traveler, unafraid of the darkness, walks toward his destiny. Each step is like the crackling of an extinguished fire, each breath a reminder of the weight of the past.

Key elements:

  • The fading era of Fire
  • The Lords of Ashes and Their Fates
  • The story of a hero who has no right to break down

The World

Deep combat

A meticulous combat system requires patience and strategy. Every opponent is a challenge.

Atmospheric visuals

From towering castles to gloomy swamps, every location is a masterpiece of dark fantasy.

Unique online system

Interconnected world

System requirements

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 7 SP1 64bit, Windows 8.1 64bit, Windows 10 64bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i3-2100 / AMD FX-6300
  • RAM: 4 GB
  • Graphics Card: NVIDIA GTX 750 Ti / ATI Radeon HD 7950
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Disk space: 25 GB

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 7 SP1 64bit, Windows 8.1 64bit, Windows 10 64bit
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-3770 / AMD FX-8350
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Graphics Card: NVIDIA GTX 970 / ATI Radeon R9 series
  • DirectX: Version 11
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection
  • Disk space: 25 GB
